All about Pancakes

Fluffy, sugary, sticky, and thick, pancakes are the best and one of the most populare breakfast meals. It is delicious and very easy to make! Pancakes are also a great meal for any time of the day. No mattter is it is for breakfass, lunch, or dinner, or even a snack. It ia also even better with fruit, syrup, and butter to top it.

FUN FACTS ABOUT PANCAKES

  • 1) The first pancakes were called Alita Dolcia ("another sweet" in Latin) and were made by Romans in the 1st Century AD.
  • 2) The world's biggest pancake measured 49+ feet in diameter and weighed 6,614 pounds! WOW!!
  • 3) The world’s most expensive pancake is available for $200.00 at Opus in the Printworks in England. Ingredients include Madagascan vanilla pods and 23-carat gold leaf.
  • 4) The record for the world’s highest pancake toss belongs to Dominic Cuzzacrea, who flipped a pancake 31 feet (and 1 inch) into the air in November 2010.
  • 5) The town of Olney in England hosts an annual pancake race in celebration of “Pancake Day.” They've done it every year since 1445.
