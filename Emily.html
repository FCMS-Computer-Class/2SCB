Topic I - Clothing from the Decades

Starting with the 50s, women mainly wore narrow knee-long skirts (like a poodle skirt), blouses, and high waisted jeans. In the 60s, simplistic designs on colorful dresses were paired with stiletto heels. The groovy 70s consisted of tie-dye shirts, tightly fitting jumpsuits, with a wide leg at the bottoms, and hippie clothing. For women's fashion in the 80s, neon colored spandex and leg warmers were worn. 90s wear included modern and casual clothing such as oversized jeans and solid colors.
