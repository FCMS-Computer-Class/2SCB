The Border Collie

By: Madden Hammer :)

A Wonderful Dog

Border Collies are energetic, friendly and intellegent. They are very good with kids which makes them lovely family pets. Border Collies are medium sized dogs, with a life span of up to 17 years.

Personality...

Border collies are very friendly. They can be easily introduced to a family and other pets. They are known for their genuine happiness. These dogs are great with kids and other animals. A Border Collie will be a happy dog if they are given plenty of attention and exercise. One thing to know about this dog is their level of energy. Border collies have exrteme amounts of energy. If they are not exercised properly, they can become very unhappy dogs. They are also known for their loyalty. Border Colllies are very loyal to their owners. They were also used for herding at one piont, which makes them very protective dogs. Border Collies are great watch dogs due to their protctive nature. These dogs are also known for endurance and hard work. They love to be given jobs. A Border Collie is a hard worker and love to be praised.

Care...

These dogs (despite their level of energy) are not high maintenence pets. They do need attention, but not a ton. If this dog gets a sickness or injury, you simply take them to a vet. Luckly, the Border Collie is a healthy breed that does not commonly have health problems. Because these are smaller dogs, the food, beds, and other such items like toys, are cheaper than most dog items, which is a bonus.

Grooming...

These dogs have realativly easy coats to manage. They need an average amount of grooming. A silmple once a week brushing or bath skould keep their coat shiny and clean. If your dog gets dirty or smelly, then bathe them and brush afterwords. Now how do you brush a border collie? Well, first collect a brush (best brush for a border collie is a slick brush), then a small doggie bag (for the fur). You may want to put a leash on your dog and tether it to something to keep it from moving around too much. Now, brush the dog starting at the head and slowlly moving down the body. Once the brush is full of hair, pull it off the brush and put it in the doggie bag (this is simply to prevent a mess). Continue this proccess until you are satisfied with the look of your dog's coat!

Training...

Trainig a border collie is reltively simple due to their intellegence and loyalty. Border Collies also respond very well to praise. First, you want to keep you border collie very well exercised. An excersised border collie is a happy border collie. You will want to socialize your dog from a young age to be sure that they are good with people, especially kids. This breed can be very social and friendly, if raised properly. You will want to teach your dog the basic commands to be sure that they are obedient. These include...

  • Sit
  • Come
  • Stay
  • Roll Over
  • Heel
  • Down
If you want your border collie to be good indoors, you will want to train it for that seperately. Overall, these dogs are even better pets when trained properly. Make sure to devote some time to training this dog properly.
